Vision

We are in the business of writing tools for working with data. Our tools are characterized by compactness and portability.

We value reproducibility and privacy. Therefore, we do not offer solutions that are not tied to our infrastructure. We make great efforts to combat the complexity of software solutions, both external and internal.

Mission

Our mission is to fight complexity. We strive to solve business problems not by bloating the code base, but by radically reducing it. Our experience shows that re-engineering processes and moving away from high-growth technologies can reduce the size of a solution by hundreds, and the internal and external complexity of a product by many times. As a result, in the long term, this allows us to radically reduce the costs of infrastructure, support, and maintenance of the solution.

Solutions

Depending on the needs of the business, we offer the following levels of problem solving:

  1. System programming in the Hare language;
  2. Application programming in the Red language;
  3. Development of DSL languages to deal with domain complexity.
